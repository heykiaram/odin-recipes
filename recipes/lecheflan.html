Leche Flan

Filipino leche flan takes Spanish creme caramel and levels it up. While the original Spanish recipe makes the dessert with fresh milk or cream, a Filipino recipe will swap out those ingredients for sweetened condensed milk. This swap gies the flan a heavier, denser texture than regular creme caramel. It tastes like a light dulce de leche caramel that melts in your mouth!

Ingredients

Leche Flan

  • granulated sugar
  • water
  • egg yolks
  • sweetened condensed milk
  • evaporated milk
  • fresh zest from two medium oranges
  • a pinch of kosher salt

Steps

  1. Make the caramel.
  2. Steam the flan, ensuring that the center still jiggles and wobbles.
