Ensaymada

Ensaymadas are a filipino pastry made with brioche dough that is rolled with butter and sugar to make a small bun. Each bun is then topped with more butter, sugar, and a special type of Edam cheese called queso de bola. They're often eaten as breakfast or a sweet afternoon snack in the Philippines.

Ingredients

Ensaymada Rolls

  • whole milk
  • active dry yeast
  • granulated sugar
  • all-purpose flour
  • kosher salt
  • room-temperature egg yolks
  • vegetable shortening

Filling, Frosting, and Topping

  • butter
  • granulated sugar
  • grated mild cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the filling, shape, and bake the buns.
